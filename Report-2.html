ADDITIONAL INFORMATION Other queries that some of our patients have, are being answered below.

Can an attendant stay with me in the hospital?
During the patient’s hospitalization period, one attendant can stay with the patient in single room at no extra cost. The food and stay of the attendant would be complimentary. In twin sharing room, two patients and two attendants can be accommodated with a common sharing bathroom.

What would be the costs of accommodation outside the hospital, as I will need to stay in hotel after completing my treatment?
The cost of treatment that has been provided to you does not include the cost of stay outside hospital. If the accommodation is requested by you (near the hospital), it will be subject to availability. Hotel accommodation may range from USD 50-80 per night.

What is the payment policy? A deposit of 80% of the cost estimate needs to be made by the patient at the time of admission. If the actual cost of treatment is lower than the amount submitted, it will be refunded at the time of discharge.

What additional documents and information do you require from my doctor? Please carry all your previous medical reports including X-Ray, MRI, CT scans (films and reports), ultrasound images, blood investigation reports, discharge summary (If any) and other relevant documents.

How soon can the investigations be scheduled? We ensure a minimal waiting period for our international patients. The treatment process is started as soon as you are admitted under the treating doctor.

Do I require a visa for India and will any assistance be provided by the hospital? Yes, for getting treatment in India, the patient requires a medical visa and the attendant would need a medical attendant visa.
